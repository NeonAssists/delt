Questions

Before you ask

Is my data actually private?

Yes. Delt runs entirely on your computer. Your files, credentials, and conversations are stored locally at ~/.delt/. The only thing that leaves your machine is the conversation text sent to Claude's API — same as using claude.ai directly. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party servers.

Do I need a paid Claude account?

No. The free Claude account works. You'll hit usage limits faster, but everything functions. Pro ($20/mo) or Max ($100-200/mo) plans give you more usage and faster responses.

What's the difference between Delt and ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a chatbot in a browser tab. Delt is an AI that runs on your computer and connects to your actual tools — it can read your email, push code, manage your calendar, and edit your files. It does things, not just talks about them.

How do integrations work?

Delt uses Model Context Protocol (MCP) — an open standard that lets AI connect to tools. You click "connect" in the integrations panel and follow the setup for each service (usually an OAuth sign-in). Everything runs locally.

Does it work offline?

The Delt interface works offline (it's a PWA). But Claude AI requires an internet connection to process your messages. Your integrations (Gmail, GitHub, etc.) also need internet to sync.

Can I uninstall it?

Yes. Run the uninstall script or just delete ~/Delt and ~/.delt. It removes the auto-start service too. Clean uninstall, nothing left behind.
