Questions

Before you ask

Is my data private?

Yes. Delt runs entirely on your computer. Your files, credentials, and conversations are stored locally at ~/.delt/. The only thing that leaves your machine is conversation text sent to Claude's API — same as using claude.ai directly. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party servers.

Do I need a paid Claude account?

No. The free Claude account works. You'll hit usage limits faster, but everything functions. Pro ($20/mo) or Max ($100-200/mo) plans give you more usage and faster responses.

What integrations are available?

33 built-in integrations: Gmail, Calendar, GitHub, Slack, Notion, Stripe, Figma, Jira, Linear, Salesforce, HubSpot, Shopify, Discord, Microsoft 365, and more. Plus a Custom API Hub — if a service has an API, you can connect it.

Can I uninstall it?

Yes. Run the uninstall script or just delete ~/Delt and ~/.delt. It removes the auto-start service too. Clean uninstall, nothing left behind.
