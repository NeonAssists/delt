Questions

Before you ask

Is my data actually private?

Yes. Delt runs entirely on your computer. Your files, credentials, and conversations are stored locally at ~/.delt/. The only thing that leaves your machine is the conversation text sent to Claude's API — same as using claude.ai directly. No analytics, no tracking, no third-party servers.

Do I need a paid Claude account?

No. The free Claude account works. You'll hit usage limits faster, but everything functions. Pro ($20/mo) or Max ($100-200/mo) plans give you more usage and faster responses.

What's the difference between Delt and ChatGPT?

ChatGPT gives you answers. Delt takes action — it reads your email, manages your calendar, edits your files, and handles tasks. It's like having an assistant, not just a search bar.

How do integrations work?

Click "connect" inside Delt and sign in to each service (like you would any app). That's it — Delt remembers the connection and can use it from then on.

Does it work offline?

The Delt interface works offline (it's a PWA). But Claude AI requires an internet connection to process your messages. Your integrations (Gmail, GitHub, etc.) also need internet to sync.

Can I use it on my phone?

Yes. Click the phone icon in Delt to generate a QR code. Scan it with your phone camera (same WiFi network). Your conversation transfers instantly — no app install needed. Your phone is just a remote screen. Everything still runs on your computer.

How many integrations does it support?

33 built-in integrations plus a Custom API Hub for anything else. If a service has an API, you can connect it. Gmail, GitHub, Slack, Notion, Stripe, Salesforce, Shopify, and more — all one-click connect.

Does it stay up to date?

Yes. Delt checks for updates automatically and installs them in the background. You always have the latest features and fixes without doing anything.

Can I uninstall it?

Yes. Run the uninstall script or just delete ~/Delt and ~/.delt. It removes the auto-start service too. Clean uninstall, nothing left behind.
