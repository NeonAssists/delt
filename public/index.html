One-time sign in

Delt needs to connect to your Claude account once. After this, you're signed in forever — it saves your credentials on this computer.

Click the button. Here's exactly what happens:

1 A Terminal window opens (black screen with text — that's normal)
2 Your browser opens to Claude's login page
3 Sign in with your Claude account (free or paid — either works)
4 Come back here — this page detects it automatically. You'll never do this again.

Look for the Terminal window that just opened (check your dock/taskbar). It will open your browser to sign in. Once done, come back here — this page updates on its own.
